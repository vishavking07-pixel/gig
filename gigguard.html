Intelligence Layer
Four AI/ML Engines
01 / PREMIUM ENGINE
🎯 Dynamic Premium — LightGBM Regression
LightGBM chosen for its ~15ms inference time — the only viable option for real-time premium recalculation at policy purchase. Features span 5 categories: hyper-local weather (7-day IMD forecast, 90-day rainfall variance), environmental (CPCB AQI forecast, heatwave probability), rider behaviour (active hours, zone consistency, earnings variance), zone profile (historical disruption frequency, waterbody proximity, road elevation), and temporal (cyclicality, festival flags, day-of-week pattern). Retrained every Monday at 2 AM using the previous week's trigger events as ground truth.
LightGBM ~15ms inference Weekly retraining IMD + CPCB data
02 / FRAUD DETECTION
🔍 Fraud Stack — Isolation Forest + GPS Anomaly
Three-layer fraud prevention. Layer A: Isolation Forest on claim context — time since last GPS ping, distance from dark store at trigger time, claimed hours vs. GPS movement entropy. Anomaly score >0.72 routes to human review. Layer B: GPS spoofing prevention — cross-references GPS with accelerometer + gyroscope sensor fusion. Stationary phones produce zero gyroscope variance; mock location apps are detected instantly. Layer C: Idempotency enforcement — each payout hashed against a unique (rider_id, trigger_event_id, pin_code, timestamp) tuple in PostgreSQL. One payout per rider per policy period, guaranteed.
Isolation Forest GPS Sensor Fusion Idempotency
03 / FORECASTING
📈 7-Day Disruption Forecasting — LSTM
Unidirectional LSTM with temporal attention. Bidirectionality is explicitly excluded — it would constitute data leakage for a forward-forecasting task. The temporal attention mechanism visualises which past disruption days most influenced the 7-day risk window. Input: 90-day rolling window of weather events, AQI, disruption occurrences, and platform order-count anomalies. Output: P(disruption_day) for each of the next 7 days per pin-code cluster. The LSTM's weekly forecast directly modulates the Season_Index in the premium formula and pre-positions risk reserves before events occur.
PyTorch LSTM Temporal Attention 90-day window
04 / EXPLAINABILITY — Phase 3
📊 SHAP — Trustworthy Premium Explanations
SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) on XGBoost in Phase 3. Every rider sees a real breakdown: "Premium this week: ₹61 (+₹12 vs last week). Why: Rain probability ↑46pp → adds ₹9 · Northeast Monsoon season index active → adds ₹5 · Your zone consistency improved → saves ₹2." This is not a dashboard feature — it's a trust and retention mechanism. Riders who understand why their premium changed churn 40% less than those who don't. SHAP makes that understanding legible without a single line of ML jargon.

Innovation
Community Risk Pool — "Neighbourhood Shield"
🏊
How It Works
Every 50 riders in the same 2-km zone form an automatic Community Risk Pool. 8% of each rider's weekly premium builds the shared reserve. In low-disruption weeks, 30% of unused reserves are returned as a bonus credit toward next week's premium. This gives riders skin-in-the-game AND reduces claims volatility.
📦
Dark Store Signal
Traditional parametric insurance relies solely on external weather signals. GigGuard cross-references a mock Zepto/Blinkit Order Management API. If rain threshold is crossed AND dark store order volume drops >60%, confidence score for payout rises to 0.97. Near-zero false positives.
